Plera Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5/5/2024

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from our users. We do not require any form of registration or personal data input. Our app operates without any user-specific data collection.

2. Information We Store

The only data we store locally on your device pertains to the operational settings of the app itself, such as preferences or configurations you choose within the app. These settings are solely to enhance your user experience and do not contain personal details.

3. Use of Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. Settings stored on your device are only used to maintain your preferences within the app.

4. Sharing of Information

As we do not collect any personal information, there is nothing to share with third parties.

5. Data Security

We take the security of the data stored by the app seriously, even though it does not contain personal information. We implement various security measures to protect the integrity of app settings stored on your device.
